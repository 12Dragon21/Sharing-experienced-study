Post by edwilliams16 » Fri Dec 29, 2023 1:15 am

Assuming you are using Part Design, you can do it with Pads and Pockets from the XY-Plane. Padding up the first sketch gives the hex part. Then I pad down to make the rectangle with a through hole, and finally a pocket to remove the corners. I make use of the external geometry tool in Sketcher to tie together the circle radii.
